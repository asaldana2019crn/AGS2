Frequent questions

1.What is the APP?

The company offers its users a virtual meeting platform between people who offer and / or exchange. Each owner of a toy is the one who decides whether he wants to give it away or exchange it to the user who requests it.

2.How is the delivery or exchange of toys?

Through a chat and action buttons, users are the ones who independently and based on their criteria and decision specify the characteristics of the product or service and agree to resolve doubts and close the delivery.

3.Service use process

- The platform may request a physical location from which to operate.
- To insert article publications on the platform, the user must previously register on it.
- Once registered, from the main page of the platform, the user can access both gifts and items.
- The user may receive notifications in the event that another user is interested in a toy.
- The user can insert as many publications as they want on the platform.
- The publications may remain published on the platform for an indefinite period from their publication.
- Users can interact with each other through a chat window and, if both users agree, exchange the product.
