The Company is not obligated to previously review the content of any publication, therefore any publication that appears on the Platform should not be understood as approved by the Company or that said publication complies with the terms of the GENERAL CONDITIONS OF USE.

In this sense, it must be the users themselves who report to COMPARTE any user or suspicious behavior or that contravenes the Terms and Conditions. In the event of a complaint by a user or on the Company's own initiative, it reserves the right, exercisable at any time and in a discretionary manner, to:

- Reject any publication.
- Delete any publication from the platform.
- Modify those fields of your publication that do not comply with the standards of insertion of the platform, all this in order to give greater quality to it.

2.Tips

- Do not create multiple accounts, false profiles.
- Do not provide more personal and contact information than that requested by the platform at any time.
- Interact with users respectfully and in relation to the purpose of the platform.
- Do not link to images of other publications or copy the full text of another publication.
